Essential Ramadan Duas

Short supplications to read throughout the blessed month.

1. Dua for Suhoor (Intention)

وَبِصَوْمِ غَدٍ نَّوَيْتُ مِنْ شَهْرِ رَمَضَانَ

*Wa bi-sawmi ghadin nawaytu min shahri Ramadan.*

(Translation: I intend to keep the fast for tomorrow in the month of Ramadan.)

2. Dua for Iftar (Breaking Fast)

اَللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي لَكَ صُمْتُ وَ بِكَ آمَنْتُ وَ عَلَيْكَ تَوَكَّلْتُ وَ عَلَى رِزْقِكَ أَفْطَرْتُ

*Allahumma inni laka sumtu wa bika aamantu wa 'alayka tawakkaltu wa 'ala rizqika aftartu.*

(Translation: O Allah! I fasted for Your sake, and I believe in You, and I put my trust in You, and I break my fast with Your provision.)

3. Post-Iftar Dua (Simpler)

ذَهَبَ الظَّمَأُ وَابْتَلَّتِ الْعُرُوقُ وَثَبَتَ الأَجْرُ إِنْ شَاءَ اللَّهُ

*Dhahaba-zh Zhama’u, wabtallatil-‘uruuqu, wa thabatal-ajru in shaa’ Allah.*

(Translation: The thirst is gone, the veins are moistened, and the reward is confirmed, if Allah wills.)
